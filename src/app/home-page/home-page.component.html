About our company

Lamoda is one of the largest online stores in Russia and the CIS. The net value of purchases in Lamoda Group online stores in Russia, Belarus, Ukraine and Kazakhstan in 2019 amounted to 43.2 billion rubles. The total number of employees is 7 thousand people. The company has 380 own delivery points and 17 thousand partner points. The site presents more than 6 million products from 3 thousand brands.Since 2014, it has been a part of the public Global Fashion Group.

Payment and refund

All products from Lamoda catalog.kz can be ordered without paying in advance. Upon delivery of the goods, the courier makes a payment with a plastic card through a mobile terminal or accepts cash from the buyer. Company implements a flexible refund policy. Goods that do not meet the correct quality can be returned within 14 days by mail, as well as when buying at least one product from a new order: through the delivery points of the goods within 14 days and with a sales representative within 30 days.
